Base Building Guide

Server Rules for Building

  • 1 Territory per player. If you’re in a group, each member may still claim their own base.
  • Vanilla structures are allowed. Use sheds, barns, and houses creatively.
  • No unlimited resources. Nails and sheet metal cannot be purchased — find them in the world.
  • Respect PvE. Bases are for survival, storage, and creativity — not raiding.

Getting Started

To begin your base, you’ll need the basics:

  • Planks: Found at sawmills, lumber yards, and scattered buildings.
  • Nails: Loot industrial sites, garages, and sheds.
  • Sheet Metal: Rarely spawns at industrial and construction zones.

Gather enough materials to craft your territory flag, then place it to secure your building area.

Customization & Style

Homestead’s BBP system lets you add personal touches to your base:

  • Plaster Kits & Wallpapers: Customize your walls and interiors for a unique look.
  • BBP Structures: Build advanced walls, gates, towers, and more.
  • Vanilla Building: Reinforce existing structures with BBP pieces to create hybrid bases.

Storage & Furnishings

Your base isn’t just walls — it’s your stronghold. Make it functional with storage and display options:

  • Wall Lockers & Gun Mounts: Show off your weapons while keeping them secure.
  • Storage Crates & Barrels: Store bulk resources and loot safely.
  • Furniture & Decor: Add immersion with working furniture pieces and interior designs.

Tips & Strategy

  • Scout your location carefully — look for defensible terrain and nearby resources.
  • Farm planks and nails whenever possible; they’re always in demand.
  • Organize with lockers and crates so you’re not drowning in loose loot.
  • Keep your flagpole maintained or your base will start to decay.
