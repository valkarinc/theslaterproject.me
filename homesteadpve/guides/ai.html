AI Guide

Airborne Patrols

Homestead features AI-controlled aircraft that roam the skies:

  • Planes begin spawning after a short delay when players are online.
  • Up to 5 aircraft can be active at once.
  • They fly both day and night, sometimes randomly overflying players.
  • If spotted, they may drop paratrooper squads on your location.

When this happens, you’ll see warnings like “Incoming Aircraft” or “You were spotted!” — time to take cover!

Paratrooper AI

When planes drop paratroopers, expect groups of 1–4 AI soldiers. They’re armed and can be looted after defeat.

  • They react to gunfire up to 900m away.
  • They detect players at ranges of 500m+, so sneaking helps.
  • AI accuracy ranges from decent to deadly — don’t underestimate them!

Tip: Take them down quickly before they spread out and flank you.

General AI Behavior

  • Accuracy: AI aim ranges from 45% to 97% depending on difficulty.
  • Damage: AI deal slightly more than players (x1.1) but take normal damage.
  • Threat Range: They will engage if you’re within ~1000m.
  • Noise: Gunshots within ~500m may draw them to investigate.
  • Manners: AI don’t follow PvP rules — they attack if hostile.

Good to know: AI can vault obstacles and move in formations. Treat them like smart human enemies.

Survival Tips

  • When you see or hear a warning, go for cover immediately.
  • Use silenced weapons to reduce your noise footprint.
  • Travel in groups — AI can overwhelm solo players if you’re unprepared.
  • Always loot defeated AI — they carry gear, ammo, and supplies worth the fight.
