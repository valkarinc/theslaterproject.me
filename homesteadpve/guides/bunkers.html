Bunker & Alien Guide

Finding Keys

Access to Banov’s hidden bunkers requires rare keys. On Homestead PvE, you can find them:

  • In Contaminated Zones guarded by toxic gas and infected.
  • On Alien Queens — deadly bosses with powerful loot.
  • Inside other bunkers, tucked away as chained progression rewards.

Each key unlocks a different bunker with unique extraterrestrial technology.

Blue Key – Area 01

Opens the first bunker. Inside lies a laboratory studying alien technology. Scientists here worked on enigmatic extraterrestrial weapons, blending unknown energy sources with human firearms.

Red Key – Area 02

Unlocks a bunker dedicated to ammunition development. Classified reports reveal they were designing bullets and shells specifically engineered to damage alien lifeforms and weaponry.

Yellow Key – Area 03

This hidden lab specialized in advanced optics. Expect to find research on scopes, targeting systems, and visionary alien tech adapted into DayZ-compatible gear.

Green Nano Key – Area 04

The most mysterious bunker. Scientists were developing a Nano Suit, capable of resisting alien attacks and providing protection against UFO combat scenarios.

Aliens & Threats

Alien encounters are a defining part of the Homestead PvE experience:

  • Alien Queens: The toughest bosses in Banov. They drop keys and alien artifacts.
  • UFO Sightings: Rare events where strange lights appear in the sky — often followed by danger.
  • Infected Hybrids: Mutated enemies inside bunkers guarding the most precious loot.

Pro Tip: Always bring NBC gear and strong weapons when exploring bunkers — alien zones are deadly.

Homestead Lore

Banov was once a thriving region — scattered towns, rolling farmlands, and industrial centers that fed the world. But that changed when the skies split open. Strange lights appeared at night, followed by sickness, mutation, and an uncanny silence in once-bustling cities. The government’s answer was secrecy: bunkers buried deep below the surface, each tasked with researching alien artifacts that had rained from the heavens.

Inside those labs, scientists uncovered technology far beyond human understanding. Weapons, optics, even experimental suits — all meant to prepare mankind for something greater… or more terrifying. But the research never stayed contained. Contamination leaked into the wild, spawning hybrid creatures and alien queens that now stalk Banov’s forests and ruins.

Survivors call this new world Homestead. Not for comfort, but for defiance — the last chance to carve out safety in the ashes of civilization. Here, every key, every bunker, every scrap of alien tech tells part of the story. The fight isn’t just against hunger or disease, but against forces from beyond the stars.

Homestead PvE is more than survival. It’s the frontier of humanity’s last stand — a place where courage, community, and resilience define who makes it to tomorrow.
