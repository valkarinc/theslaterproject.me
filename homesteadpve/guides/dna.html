DNA Workbench Guide

Steps at a Glance

  1. Gather the required kits (Workbench, Helping Hands, Soldering Station).
  2. Collect crafting materials to assemble the Workbench.
  3. Use the Workbench to craft DNA keycards with batteries, flux, and solder.
  4. Use keycards to unlock crates and vaults for exclusive loot.

Finding the Kits

Look for three kits in industrial areas, cargo containers, and high-end loot zones:

  • DNA Workbench Kit
  • Helping Hands Kit
  • Soldering Station Kit

Combine them to place the operational DNA Workbench at your base or base extension.

Crafting the Workbench

To assemble the Workbench, you’ll need:

  • 16 Bolts
  • 13 Wood Planks
  • 5 Sheet Metal
  • 1 Metal Wire
  • 1 Card Reader
  • 1 Laptop

Once crafted, you’re ready to make keycards.

Crafting DNA Keycards

At the Workbench, you’ll need:

  • 1× 40V Battery
  • Flux
  • Solder

Each keycard created is one-time use and will show “ERROR” once used in a crate or vault.

Loot Containers & Vaults

The mod includes 5 tiers of loot containers and vaults:

  • Yellow → Red: Higher color = higher loot tier
  • Illuminated at night for easy recognition
  • Vaults are multi-story with tactical layouts
