Money Making Guide

Sell .338 Weapons

The .338 caliber rifles are some of the most valuable weapons you can find. Take them to the Black Market to sell for quick and hefty profits.

Tip: Even damaged .338s fetch a solid price — never leave them behind.

Sell Attachments

Weapon attachments are always in demand. Scopes, suppressors, grips, and high-end mods can be sold at the Black Market for consistent cash flow.

Pro Move: Loot military zones and helicopter crash sites for rare optics and silencers.

Grow & Sell Weed

Cultivate weed plants to make serious money. Find seeds, set up a safe grow spot, and harvest fully grown plants to sell.

  • Plant in hidden or secure areas to avoid theft.
  • Use water and time to grow healthy crops.
  • Take the harvested weed to traders or the Black Market for high payouts.

Tip: Growing is a long-term play — but the rewards are worth it.

Mining & Refining

Mining ores and gems is another steady money maker. Collect raw materials, smelt them into ingots, and cut gems to sell for profit.

Read the Mining Guide

Strategy Tips

  • Mix fast cash (attachments, .338s) with long-term gains (weed, gems).
  • Keep some resources for crafting — don’t sell everything.
  • Team up with other survivors to split roles (farmer, miner, looter, seller).
