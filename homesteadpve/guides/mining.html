Mining Guide

Step 1: Mining Resource Nodes

Start by exploring caves, mountains, and rocky hills. Look for mineable rocks that contain valuable materials. You can find:

  • Metals: Copper, Tin, Iron, Silver, Gold, Uranium
  • Stone: Used for basic crafting and building
  • Gems: Ruby, Jade, Amethyst, Aquamarine, Amber, Turquoise, Fancy Diamonds, and more

Use your pickaxe to extract Ore Rocks and raw gems.

Step 2: Smelting in a Furnace

Once you’ve collected ores, craft a Furnace. Place your Ore Rocks inside to smelt them down into nuggets.

It takes 10 nuggets of the same type to move on to crafting ingots or bars.

Step 3: Crafting Bars & Ingots

With your nuggets ready, build an Anvil. This station lets you refine your nuggets into ingots or bars.

Bars and ingots are highly valuable for trading, crafting gear, or storing wealth safely.

Step 4: Gem Cutting

Raw gems can be taken to a Gem Cutting Station. Here’s how it works:

  • Insert your raw gem into the station.
  • Start the gem cutting process.
  • A short mini-game may appear where precision matters.
  • The outcome determines the quality of your gem:
    • Flawed Gem (50%) – lower value, but still usable.
    • Standard Gem (35%) – good quality, higher trade value.
    • Perfect Gem (15%) – rare and extremely valuable!

Pro Tips:

  • Stockpile raw gems before cutting to maximize your odds of getting Perfects.
  • Even flawed gems can still be sold — nothing goes to waste.
  • Practice the mini-game timing to increase your success with higher-quality cuts.

Extra Tips & Strategy

  • Plan mining routes around caves and mountain ranges for efficiency.
  • Sell ingots and flawed gems for steady cash flow — save Perfects for big trades.
  • Team up with friends: one mines, one smelts, one cuts gems for faster production.
  • Balance between selling and crafting — some resources are worth more if kept.
